What this documentation covers

This wiki complements the main ValueApp website by focusing on practical usage: how to work with projects, define services, interpret results, and connect the ETH Value Method to real planning processes.

The content is intended for architecture, engineering and construction professionals, as well as organisations evaluating a structured, transparent approach to time-effort and fee calculation.

Who is ValueApp for?

ValueApp is designed for planning teams and organisations that need a transparent, reproducible way to assess planning effort and discuss fees:

  • Architects
  • Civil engineers
  • Building services engineers
  • Public and private clients
  • Professional associations and working groups

In practice, many teams use ValueApp as a shared basis for negotiation, comparison of project variants and benchmarking across portfolios.
