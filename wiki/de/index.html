Explanations, examples and practical guidance for using ValueApp and the ETH Value Method in day-to-day planning work.

This wiki is for users (planning teams and clients). Technical details for embedding the widget are documented in a separate integration repository.

Why ValueApp exists – and what it is not for

Many misunderstandings happen because ValueApp is seen as a fee calculator, proposal generator, or a controlling tool. It is not.

This page explains the purpose of ValueApp, its limits, and how to use it responsibly within the planning process.
